All It Takes Is One Person To Make A Difference

A simple journey of a leaf with a purpose

What Inspired Us To Make The Change

We happened upon a fallen coconut leaf one day with one of its leaves pointing upwards. The leaves were curled like a straw and this inspired a thought, one which could turn all these fallen leaves into something that could spark change. After some research we found that most of the farmers burn these leaves in landfills or out behind their kitchens. But now, they won’t need to as they can be converted into a product that creates value and solves a wicked global problem.

But this not a story of straws. It’s about lives, precious human lives. The lives of women in rural communities. Our intention is to empower these women so that they can improve the living standards of their families. And we want them to touch lives, beyond their hearth and home. We envision these women impacting the world with the products they make. And heal it, too.

We foresee a gentleman in California or a little girl in Auckland sipping guilt-free out of a straw made by the palms of these women and out of the tall palms nurtured by Mother Earth.

That’s the story of Sunbird Straws.

Thank you for being a part of this story, too.
