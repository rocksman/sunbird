What Inspired Us To Make The Change

Here at Sunbird straws, we are driven by a shared dream for a Green tomorrow. Sustaining the environment and empowering rural communities, one green innovation at a time, lie at the heart of our philosophy. Climate change being the biggest disruptor of our times, being receptive to the needs of our planet and sustainable development for our communities become two core values for businesses. That is why we created Sunbird Straws™, from agri-waste that otherwise gets burnt, adding to the already high levels of carbon emission. Our innovation aims to curb the plastic menace endangering our oceans and aquatic life, empower rural women by creating meaningful employment opportunities and involve communities by making a switch to sustainable lifestyle more convenient through our green innovations that are simple yet powerful.
